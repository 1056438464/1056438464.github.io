win7下用xampp部署php项目

1、下载xampp安装

2、设置apache的conf文件

D:\xampp\apache\conf

修改：

DocumentRoot "D:/weikang/weikang_system/src" //你项目地址目录

   .....

添加：

在最后中添加

AddHandler application/x-httpd-php .php
    AddType   application/x-httpd-php  .php  .html  .htm
    LoadModule php7_module D:/xampp/php/php7apache2_4.dll //确定你的php版本




   ......

    AddHandler application/x-httpd-php .php
    AddType   application/x-httpd-php  .php  .html  .htm
    LoadModule php7_module D:/xampp/php/php7apache2_4.dll

参考文章：https://www.cnblogs.com/convincee/p/6636760.html

3、修改项目目录中conf文件（config.php）修改数据库地址

D:\weikang\weikang_system\src\data\config.php

$config['db']['master']['host'] = '数据库ip';
$config['db']['master']['username'] = '用户';
$config['db']['master']['password'] = '密码';
$config['db']['master']['port'] = '3306';
$config['db']['master']['database'] = 'weikang';
$config['db']['master']['charset'] = 'utf8';
$config['db']['master']['pconnect'] = 0;
$config['db']['master']['tablepre'] = 'ims_';
